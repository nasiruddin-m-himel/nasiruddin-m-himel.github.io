I'm a third-year EECE student at MIST, Bangladesh, diving deep into IC Layout, Chip Design, and the fascinating world of nanotechnology. My work spans CNFET-based SRAM architectures, TCAD simulations, and deep learning for embedded systems.

Beyond the lab, I thrive in community and leadership — currently serving as Associate Director at IEEE MIST Student Branch and Team Coordinator at MIST Robotics Club. I believe great engineering is built by great teams.

When I'm not pushing silicon boundaries, I freelance as a digital artist and once tutored high school mathematics. I find creativity in both circuits and canvas.
